PANS / PANDAS Caregiver Outcome Instrument

NROS-P is a caregiver-reported outcome tool for tracking symptom burden and treatment response in children with PANS, PANDAS, and related inflammatory brain disorders. Built on the validated PTEC framework (Vyshedskiy et al., 2025 — 101 items, Cronbach's α = 0.96) with BeaconCR longitudinal research enhancements.

I. Behavior / Mood
II. OCD
III. Anxiety
IV. Food Intake
V. Tics
VI. Cognitive / Dev
VII. Sensory
VIII. Other
IX. Sleep
X. Health
